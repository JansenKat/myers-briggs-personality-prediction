Methodology

This was a long and arduous process. Finding meaningful datapoints, exploring different models and realizing predicting everything in a single model doesn't work. Then splitting up the attributes and continuing the process times 4. The end result is 4 models, each to predict 1 attribute of the Myers-Briggs personality type and combine them later.

First, let's go into Myers-Briggs personality types. Myers and Briggs were 2 intelligent women in the early 1900s who noticed different people thought in different ways. They decided to analyse and classify the different people around them. During WWII, their published research and personality types were used to identify the best job type for women to do while the men were off at war. Now, there are 5 attributes and the test is more for fun and self-understanding than finding your place in society.
